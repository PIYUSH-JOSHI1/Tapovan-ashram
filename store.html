Shipping & Returns

Shipping Policy

We ship to addresses within India and internationally. Domestic orders typically arrive within 5-7 business days, while international orders may take 10-15 business days. Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on weight and destination.

Return Policy

If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, you may return it within 14 days of receipt. Items must be unused and in their original packaging. Please note that shipping costs for returns are the responsibility of the customer.

Quality Assurance

All products in our store are carefully selected for quality and authenticity. Many items are handcrafted by skilled artisans or produced using traditional methods. We stand behind the quality of every item we sell.
