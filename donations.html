Your Contribution Makes a Difference

ॐ

Tapovan Ashram operates entirely on the generous contributions of supporters like you. Your donations help us maintain our facilities, support our resident spiritual teachers, provide scholarships for sincere seekers with limited means, and extend our outreach programs to the wider community.

In the spirit of seva (selfless service), we invite you to participate in our mission of spreading spiritual wisdom and creating a more peaceful, harmonious world.

The Impact of Your Support

ॐ

Ashram Maintenance

Your donations help us maintain and improve our meditation halls, accommodation facilities, gardens, and other spaces that provide a conducive environment for spiritual practice.

Spiritual Education

Contributions support our programs, workshops, and retreats, allowing us to share authentic spiritual teachings with seekers from all walks of life.

Community Service

We extend our service to the local community through educational initiatives, health camps, and environmental projects, all made possible by your generosity.

Scholarships

Your support enables us to offer scholarships to sincere spiritual seekers who may not have the financial means to participate in our programs.

Ways to Contribute

ॐ

One-Time Donation

Make a one-time contribution of any amount to support our mission. Every donation, regardless of size, makes a meaningful difference.

Monthly Giving

Become a sustaining supporter by setting up a recurring monthly donation. This provides reliable support that helps us plan and grow our programs.

Sponsor a Program

Sponsor a specific program, retreat, or workshop. Your contribution will help cover the costs of organizing and conducting these transformative events.

In-Kind Donations

Contribute materials, supplies, or professional services that support our ashram operations. We welcome donations of items such as:

  • Books for our spiritual library
  • Yoga mats and meditation cushions
  • Organic gardening supplies
  • Kitchen equipment
  • Professional services (accounting, legal, etc.)

Payment Methods

ॐ

Credit/Debit Card

Make a secure online payment using your credit or debit card. We accept all major cards including Visa, Mastercard, and RuPay.

Bank Transfer

Transfer your donation directly to our bank account. Contact us for our bank details or find them in the donation form.

UPI/Mobile Wallets

Use popular Indian payment methods like UPI, Paytm, Google Pay, or PhonePe for quick and convenient donations.

Check/Money Order

Mail a check or money order to our address. Please make it payable to "Tapovan Ashram Trust".

Tax Benefits

ॐ

Tapovan Ashram is a registered charitable organization. Donations made to the ashram are eligible for tax benefits under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act in India. For international donors, please check the tax laws in your country regarding charitable donations.

After making your donation, you will receive an official receipt that you can use for tax purposes. If you have any questions about tax deductions, please contact our office.
